If you've tried everything, you're not the problem.

Maybe you've tried diet plans from Instagram, followed strict meal charts, or searched "what should I eat?" and ended up more confused than before. Maybe you're managing diabetes, PCOS, weight concerns, cholesterol, blood pressure, or digestive issues and you need clear, practical guidance that fits your routine and the food your family already eats.

As a clinical dietitian, I don't believe in one-size-fits-all diets. Your nutrition plan should consider your medical history, lifestyle, food preferences, work schedule, budget, and health goals. Whether it includes daal, roti, rice, sabzi, or your favourite home-cooked meals, the goal is to help you eat better in a way that feels realistic, sustainable, and medically appropriate.

Services

Care for the conditions that diet actually changes.

  1. 01

    Weight Management

    Sustainable weight loss or healthy weight gain — protein-forward plans with realistic portions, not starvation. Patients have lost 20+ kg without giving up everyday Pakistani food.

  2. 02

    Metabolic Health

    Diabetes, pre-diabetes, and insulin resistance. Plans designed to stabilise blood sugar through meal timing, carbohydrate balance, and foods you already know how to cook.

  3. 03

    PCOS & Hormonal Health

    Nutrition therapy for PCOS and hormonal imbalance — managing insulin resistance, inflammation, and weight in a way that works with your cycle, not against it.

  4. 04

    Cardiovascular Nutrition

    Diet management for hypertension and heart health — practical sodium, fat, and fibre strategies adapted to desi cooking, so the whole family can eat from the same pot.

  5. 05

    Clinical & Post-surgical Nutrition

    Medical nutrition therapy for recovery after surgery or illness, including tube-feeding plans, plus specialised paediatric and geriatric nutrition support.

  6. 06

    Sports & Fitness Nutrition

    Fuelling plans for gym-goers and athletes — building muscle, cutting fat, and timing meals around training without expensive imported supplements.

Not sure which applies to you? Send a WhatsApp message and describe what you're dealing with — you'll get a straight answer.
